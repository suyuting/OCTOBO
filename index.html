alt.ctrl.GDC 2016!

OCTOBO was selected to be showcased at GDC (Game Developers Conference) in San Francisco in March 2016! Come visit us if you have the chance!

We will be at alt.ctrl.GDC, the on-site exhibition for alternative control schemes and interactions in games, organized by John Polson.

  • OCTOBO is a reactive plush octopus toy that comes with a storybook.

    As parents and children read the book together, they discover what OCTOBO wants, give him things they find in the book, interact with OCTOBO, and see him respond to them.

    He has a tablet display on his head and soft sensors throughout his body.
